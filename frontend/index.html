Sankey Scorecard

Scoring Methodology

Overview

Teams are scored on two dimensions for a composite score of 0–100.

Dimension 1: Categorization Rate (70 points)

Percentage of issues with the Activity Type field set. Measures whether the team has adopted the Sankey framework by categorizing their work. All six categories (including Associate Wellness) count.

Formula: (categorized issues / total issues) × 70

Dimension 2: Distribution Alignment (30 points)

How well the team's work distribution across priority categories matches the target allocation. Associate Wellness issues are excluded from this calculation.

Asymmetric Penalty Weights

Deviations from target are penalized asymmetrically by category priority:

Over-allocating to high-priority categories is penalized less; under-allocating to high-priority categories is penalized more.

Aggregation

Pillar and organization scores are issue-count-weighted averages. Teams with no data are excluded from aggregation.
